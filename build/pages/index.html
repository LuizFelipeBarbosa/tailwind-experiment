Synopsis

Desert Flowers follows Dr. Cinthya Martinez and other undocumented activists as they challenge the largest immigrant prison in California, Adelanto. Through first-hand accounts from those who have put their bodies on the line, we investigate the impact of deportation and detention on communities. The film centers feminist and queer undocumented voices spearheading a movement to abolish ICE and all border prisons.
